Additional Programs

Special Activity Classes

Enable children to widen their experiences by learning new skills and developing their emerging talents. These classes foster individuality as well as an awareness of social connectedness. Classes offered include sports (basketball, soccer, judo, and swimming), dance (hip hop, flamenco, tap), and theater (Adventures in Theater and Shakespeare). Also offered each year are cooking, computer instruction, and private.

Vacation Camp

Vacation Camp is available during most school vacations, depending on volume of need.
