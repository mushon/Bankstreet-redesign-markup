Our Location

Where is Bankstreet Located?

Bank Street College is located in a bustling family and university neighborhood four blocks from Columbia University and one block from St. John the Divine, the largest Gothic cathedral in North America. We are a short bus ride from Lincoln Center and Fifth Avenue. Our main building is just a few steps off Broadway, a busy avenue lined with shops and restaurants. We are conveniently located near subway lines, bus routes, and major roads.

From the NORTH:

  • Take the 95th/96th Street exit of the Henry Hudson Parkway (West Side Highway).
  • Take the first exit (95th Street) and make a left at the light onto Riverside Drive and drive north.
  • Make a right onto 110th Street.
  • Take the first left and then the first right onto 112th Street.
  • The main building of Bank Street College is on your right, mid-block.
