Featured CE/CPS Course

Introduction to Adult Learning

This workshop through the Career Development focus at CPS is an introduction to learning in adulthood. It is geared towards administrators looking to create better environments that foster adult learning as well as parents who want to explore how their own learning impacts their child's learning. The role of experience and learning styles as well as the complexity and transformational possibilities in adult learning will each be considered. read more

Course timetable: July 7 - August 2 Prof. Katherine O'Donnell, EdD $350 (not offered for credit) Registration deadline in 5 days
