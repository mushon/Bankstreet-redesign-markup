Elective credits may be taken at any time during the program. Popular electives include LEAD 560: Literacy and Leadership (offered in Summer 1), LEAD 871: Special Education Leadership I: Implementation of IDEA and LEAD 872: Special Education Leadership II: Leading Inclusive Communities of Learners (times vary), and LEAD 514N: Staff Development (offered over two spring weekends through New Perspectives). Students sometimes take courses through other leadership programs (with special permission) and through General and Special Education.

This chart shows how the program may be completed in the timeframe indicated, but it is only a sample, meant to reflect the versatility of the programs at Bank Street.

Once you are admitted to the College, you and your program director or advisor will lay out an academic plan that both meets your educational goals and fits your lifestyle. Our financial aid counselors are happy to advise you about your financial needs.
