Apr 12

Join us for the 2011 Trustees’ Dinner where we can meet again, toast to Bankstreet’s past achievements and secure Bankstreet’s future.

Dr. Bettye Fletcher Comer is a Bank Street Trustee and Connecticut educational leader.

Dr. James P. Comer is an award winning child psychiatrist, writer, professor of psychiatry at the Yale Child Study Center, Director of the Child Study Center's Comer School Development Project, and Associate Dean at Yale Medical School
